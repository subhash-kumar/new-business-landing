Step 1

Sign up to register for the NIMS Exam.

Submit your basic details like Name, Email ID, Mobile Number, State, Gender & Password. Your Email ID will work as your Username for the portal, and the password will be set by you upon registration.

Step 2

Take the CUCET.

After completing your candidate profile for NIMS, proceed to choose the date for your examination to take the NIMS Exam.

Step 3

Admissions & Scholarship.

You are now eligible to take admission at NIMS University, Rajasthan, and apply for merit-based scholarships once your result is announced.
